# 🎂 Cake Matching

## Project Goal  
Create a cake showcase website focused on personalized flavors.  
The site introduces various combinations of cake sizes and flavors to help users understand product features and initiate custom inquiries.  
It also generates a cake preview image for ordering.  
The website aims to enhance the brand’s professional image, attract customers with specific taste preferences, and increase customer loyalty.

---

## Target Users and Their Needs

### Flavor-Conscious Consumers  
1. Explore flavor and filling combinations with shop recommendations  
2. Browse products by cake size and ingredient types  
3. Easily place orders or start custom inquiries  
4. Discover new or seasonal limited flavors  

### Young Consumers  
1. Loyal to certain classic flavors  
2. Interested in checking out new or limited-edition flavors  
3. Want a quick way to reorder or share with friends  
4. Prefer minimal cake designs with low-sugar, healthier recipes  

---

## Key Deliverables  
- A clean and modern cake showcase website  
- Filtering options for flavor combinations and cake size descriptions  
- An interactive preview interface that displays cake slice color/flavor combinations, allowing users to select by swiping left or right  
- A design that emphasizes "quality ingredients" and "flavor customization" to build a memorable brand impression  

---

## Special Requirements  
Website content must follow food industry standards:  
- No exaggerated claims  
- No misleading information  

---

## Expected Results  
- Help users quickly understand the customization process and flavor highlights  
- Increase customer trust and encourage repeat purchases  
- Bring more custom orders and promote the brand effectively
